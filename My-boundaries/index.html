What boundaries actually are?

Boundary is a loudly defined rule for other people. Which no one should cross, and which will help you avoid events, you don't like. For example, your boundary could be "Working 8 hours per day". If you define something like that, it is your responsibility to keep people away from crossing it!

Why should we define them? And take care of them?

Boundaries give us space and freedom to rest. They save us from saying directly "No". And defend us against things, which we don't want!

When you're starting a new friendship. Create clear boundaries of what the other person can expect from you. It will help you to get along with each other!
