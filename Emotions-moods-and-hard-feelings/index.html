😀🙃 Emotions, moods, and hard feelings

🔒Locked in the house

It happened! I'm on COVID19 quarantine 🦠😷 . I thought I could handle this better. I was wrong❗I was confident about it because I've spent one and half years in lockdown, and I dealt with it surprisingly well. I've changed many habits, learned new things, etc. After all, lockdown had a positive effect on me. Now I had a virus for 1 week, and I'm starting to get crazy in a home.

What has changed?

After long thinking (about 5 minutes😝), I've found a few reasons for my quarantine bad outcomes:

  1. I'm totally locked. During the last pandemic, I went outside every day 🌲🌳
  2. I literally have a COVID 😷
  3. I've become more emotional, and I've got more deep, meaningful friendships.

What basic emotions do we have?

Depending on a model, we can say about various BASIC emotions.

For me, there are 7 basic emotions:

  • Love ♥️,
  • Fear 😨,
  • Anger 😠,
  • Sadness 😞,
  • Happiness 😼,
  • Surprise 😯,
  • Disgust 😖.

We rarely think, which emotions we feel! But why should we? There are a few reasons:

  1. You will understand how you work. What to do to be motivated. What to do to be happy and so on.
  2. You will understand how people feel. You will find more easily to talk with 'em

What have I changed when I realize something is wrong?

I've started to think about what I'm feeling right now. Why do I feel those emotions? How I can change this. Then I started changing it! Easy? Yes, but actually not...

It isn't a universal rule to change how you feel, but it worked for me...

The first thought I came up with, that I had to talk with someone ❗ So I made it as I thought. I texted a friend of mine, and I said what I felt. She helped me out, but not completely. Often to deal with a problem, we have to process it alone.

So I decided to slow down for a one day. And it worked out‼️ I started to thinking about my life. I listened amazing podcast about philosophy(🤔 Filozofia po prostu Czym jest filozofia?, Filozofia po prostu Utylitaryzm), self development(❓Z pasją o mocnych stronach 170 Ulubione pytania❓), and ➖ Minimalism(315 Expired Things The minimalists)

Few conlusions for ya 😉

Take care of yourself. Talk with your friend about problems 💬 Stop for a moment 🛑 and think about your feelings. Start doing what you love ♥️
